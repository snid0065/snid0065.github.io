About Me

My name is Emma Snider. I have been around the sun 18 times so far, and throughout those 18 years I have pursued many hobbies. Mostly crafty things like sewing, crocheting, beading, painting and such; most outstanding though is the years I spent as a competitive horseback rider. I have been riding since I could walk but hung up my helmet before moving to Ottawa to pursue a college degree and career.

"Emma and Athena 1.40m" photo by Leah Taggart

I am super happy to be here pursuing another side of my interests. Since I started high school my electives have been split between technology and arts credits. It was really hard to be a girl in tech classes. I was usually the only one in each of those classes, especially my computer science classes, but persevered through and came out on top of every class, even graduating with a 95% average. Now that I am here, I still get to be creative all the while playing on computers. It's pretty great.
